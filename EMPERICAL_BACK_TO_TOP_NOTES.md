# Emperical Back-to-Top + Edition Label Notes

This patch keeps the homepage as the publication front door and preserves the manual editorial edition system.

Changes:
- Homepage heading changed from “Current Edition · May 30, 2026” to “Latest Edition · May 30, 2026”.
- Footer edition label was updated to match.
- Added a visible fixed “↑ Top” button that returns readers to the page header/navigation.
- No story text, archive structure, source links, or update-system rules were changed.

Reason:
Emperical pages are long by design. A persistent back-to-top button improves navigation for readers who reach later stories.
