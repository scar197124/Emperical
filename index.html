Good Samaritans · Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, United States

Restaurant Owner Found $12,000 and Returned Every Dollar

While clearing old cabinets from a restaurant he had purchased, Sak Yiengjuntuek found a sunglasses case holding $12,000. He immediately tried to locate the former owner and used local television coverage when the old phone number no longer worked.

Why It Matters

The story is a direct example of integrity when nobody is forcing the right decision. Returning the money protected another person from a major loss and strengthened trust around a new local business.

Ripple Effect

One honest choice became a public reminder that found property still belongs to someone—and that local media can help complete an act of restitution.
